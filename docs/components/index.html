Our productions

We work with everyone from disability organisations to mainstream dance theatre companies, presenting at conferences with the British Council, and performing at festivals and events locally, regionally, nationally and internationally

Work with us

We deliver world-class workshops based on contract improvisation, movement and somatic practices for visually impaired, deaf and disabled people all over the world. We can create bespoke workshops for groups in arts organisations, care centres, health and education settings.
